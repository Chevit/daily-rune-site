Frequently Asked Questions

How many readings do I get for free?

Free users receive a set number of readings per day. Once the daily limit is reached, you can wait until the next day or unlock unlimited readings with a one-time premium purchase.

How do I unlock unlimited readings?

Tap the unlock button on the Home screen or when you reach your daily limit. The premium upgrade is a one-time purchase — you will never be charged again.

I purchased premium but it's not showing up

Try the following steps:

  • Close and reopen the App
  • Make sure you are connected to the internet
  • Check that you are signed into the same Google Play / App Store account used for the purchase
  • Uninstall and reinstall the App — your purchase will be restored automatically

If the issue persists, please contact us with your order number.

How does the shake-to-draw feature work?

On the rune board screen, give your phone a short shake and the runes will be shuffled. Tap a rune to select it. In three-rune mode, select three runes one at a time.

What is a reversed rune?

Some runes have a reversed (merkstave) meaning when drawn upside-down. The App randomly determines reversal for each rune drawn, adding depth to your reading.

How do I change the reading style?

Go to Settings and toggle between Traditional and Casual reading styles. Traditional uses classical rune interpretations; Casual uses a more modern, conversational tone.

How do I turn off analytics?

Go to Settings and toggle off Help improve the app. This immediately disables all analytics data collection.

How do I turn off personalised ads?

On Android: Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalisation.
On iOS: Settings → Privacy → Tracking and disable tracking for Daily Rune.

The app is crashing or not working correctly

Try these steps first:

  • Update the App to the latest version from the store
  • Restart your device
  • Uninstall and reinstall the App

If the problem continues, please email us with a description of the issue and your device model and OS version.
